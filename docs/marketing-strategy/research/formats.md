# Formats

Overall approach: adapt and combine different formats for different pillars of the strategy.

- Shared:
  - Overall positioning / brand voice
    - [Obviously Awesome - Positioning by April Dunford](https://www.aprildunford.com/books)
      - Why: seems decent
  - Personas
    - One per pillar or 2-3?
  - Messaging?
  - Measurement
    - OKR
      - Why: decent, familiar with lots of people, will trickle down from top to bottom
- Pillar: Product
  - Jobs-to-be-Done
    - Why: familiarity. Will naturally lead to segmented messaging for the different use cases.
  - SWOT
    - Why: familiarity
- Pillar: Foundation
  - [Stakeholder Map / Mendelow’s Matrix](https://www.professionalacademy.com/blogs/mendelows-matrix-marketing-theories/)
  - Theory of Change
    - Why: awareness from work
  - SWOT
  - If there is time: [Donor Pyramid](https://kindsight.io/resources/blog/donor-pyramid-guide/)
- Pillar: Community?
  - Open source + other forms of community contributions
  - Contributor Journey Map
  - SWOT
  - If there is time: [Onion model](https://www.researchgate.net/figure/The-Onion-Model-of-an-Open-Source-Community_fig1_45922099)
