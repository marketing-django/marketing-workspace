# Django marketing workspace

A shared workspace to support our work on a new marketing strategy for Django and follow-on marketing activities.

## Sub-projects

Organize information across three layers:

1. [Knowledge base](./knowledge-base/): general-purpose information about Django, relevant for a wide range of projects.
2. [Marketing strategy](./marketing-strategy/): information and working documents related to marketing specifically.
3. [Website redesign](./website-redesign/): information about the website redesign project specifically.
